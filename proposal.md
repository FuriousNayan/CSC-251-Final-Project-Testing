### Project Title
* The WECIB Card Game

---

### What are you building?

* A card game, kind of like pokemon, that has a deck of WECIB Students/Teachers 
* You make your deck by a triple draft, and fight with eachother with different abilities and statuses. 
* Your end goal is to beat the other opponent, and be the WECIB Master
* 

---

### Why is this a Java 2 Project?
* Generics will be a good portion of this project, because generics was used all throughout Java 2
* It will utalize more complex class structures and data structures which we previously didn't know
* about in Java 1.
* 

### Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

* Card Game with abilities
* Players go up against each other and fight until one player is left.
* Art to complement story.
